Our Story

Kahwa was never meant to be rushed.

It started as a pot on a stovetop in Hyderabad — cardamom pods cracked by hand, saffron bloomed in warm water, and a kettle that never really went cold. Kahwa Co. is that same pot, scaled up just enough to share with a city.

We roast in small batches, source spice direct from growers we've actually met, and refuse to let a drive-thru speed us up. Good kahwa asks for four minutes. We think you have four minutes.
